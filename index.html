Who We Are

Vivid Field Advertising Co. LLC is a full-service branding and advertising solutions company specializing in exhibitions, signages, and branding activations. Headquartered in the UAE, we bring brands to life through impactful design, innovative concepts, and seamless execution.


With a strong in-house production capability and a creative team of branding experts, Vivid Field Advertising delivers end-to-end solutions — from concept development and fabrication to on-site installation. Our goal is to create immersive brand experiences that captivate audiences and elevate visibility across malls, exhibitions, outdoor spaces, and retail environments

What We Do

01

Exhibition Stands

We turn empty spaces into magnetic experiences. From sleek concepts to stunning fabrication, our exhibition stands are built to captivate and connect. Every curve, color, and corner tells your brand story — bold, beautiful, and built to impress. Step in and stand out.

02

Signage Solutions

Light it up or keep it classy — we craft signs that speak louder than words. Whether it’s indoor elegance or outdoor impact, illuminated or subtle, our signage designs lead the way. Because your first impression deserves to shine, day or night.

03

Branding Activations

We don’t just build kiosks — we build moments that stick. From promotional setups to retail takeovers, we bring brands to life with immersive activations and eye-catching displays. Let’s make your audience stop, stare, and share.

04

Creative & Production

Design, print, build, repeat — but never the same way twice. Our creative team blends imagination with precision to produce visuals that hit hard and stay memorable. From pixel to print, we make your brand impossible to ignore.

05

Corporate Gifts

We believe gifts should speak louder than logos. From premium giveaways to personalized keepsakes, we craft corporate gifts that tell your brand’s story with style. Whether it’s for clients, partners, or your own team — we make every unboxing moment feel special, thoughtful, and on-brand.

Work Process

01

Discover

We kick off by diving deep into your brand world — exploring ideas, colors, moods, and stories that define your identity. Every insight sparks a new direction, helping us see what makes you different. It’s where imagination meets intention, and the foundation for every great design begins.

02

Design

This is where concepts start to breathe. We sketch, visualize, and shape bold ideas into designs that speak and shine. Every layout, texture, and curve is chosen with purpose — blending creativity and clarity for maximum impact.

03

Develop

Our creators take over, crafting, printing, and building every element with skill and soul. It’s the magic moment when designs move from screen to reality. From structure to surface, every detail is handled with care and perfection.

04

Deliver

Execution is our grand finale. We install, refine, and light it up — turning spaces into experiences that draw every eye. From first look to final touch, we make sure your brand arrives in style, flawlessly and memorably.
